Systematic Trading

Inference Street Alpha

Inference Street Alpha is an internal, proprietary program used to guide our research and technology decisions. It is not a product we market or offer to clients. The program is rules-based and focused on liquid US equities and equity index derivatives.

Strategy in Plain Language

  • Diversified, rules-based signals across multiple time horizons, combining price action, volatility regimes, and cross-sectional relationships.
  • Focus on liquid large and mid-cap US equities and major index products to keep execution, slippage, and capacity under tight control.
  • Explicit risk budgets at the position, sector, and portfolio levels, with volatility-aware sizing and pre-defined loss limits.
  • Automatic drawdown controls, hard stop-outs on system behavior, and conservative gross and net exposure limits.
Performance context: In multi-year historical simulations, our flagship configuration has targeted low double-digit annualized returns, with single-digit to low double-digit maximum drawdowns and a Sharpe ratio above 1.0 under base-case risk settings.
All performance figures are based on internal research and backtests. They are unaudited and for illustrative purposes only. Backtested results are not indicative of future performance. Live results will differ, sometimes materially. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

How We Build & Operate

  • Proprietary research platform with version-controlled experiments, reproducible backtests, and clear promotion paths from research to live trading.
  • Data sourced from reputable market providers with validation, cleaning, and monitoring before it is used in production research or trading.
  • Continuous integration for simulations and risk tests to detect code drift, broken assumptions, or unexpected behavior before it shows up in live capital.
  • Execution and risk monitoring with real-time alerting, circuit breakers, and explicit capital preservation thresholds that halt or scale down the program if breached.
  • Operated solely on our own capital; no external investor access or allocations.
Internal program – proprietary capital only

Program Characteristics

  • Designed around clear holding-period bands and capital turnover targets, not open-ended positions.
  • Prefers frequent, smaller adjustments over infrequent, concentrated bets.
  • Built to avoid latency arms races while still respecting modern microstructure and execution quality.

This program is part of our internal research and technology stack, not a client product.

Prototype

Beats67 – AI Music Studio

Beats67 is our experimental AI music studio. It lets creators generate songs about their lives, interests, and stories — safely and playfully. Today, Beats67 is used as an internal prototype and testbed for our generative media stack.

How it Works

  • Theme-based prompts (soccer, space, friendship, school, kindness, math) with guided creativity.
  • Content filters and safety controls.
  • Simple UI that favors playful exploration over complexity.
  • Focus on creativity and learning with songs about good habits and life principles.

Stage & Roadmap

Beats67 is currently in prototype. We are testing generation models, safety-forward UX flows, and controls with a small internal group.

Future directions include a mobile or web app, classroom-friendly modes, and personalized experiences like birthday songs or “life code” tracks.

Beats67 extends Inference Street’s capabilities beyond markets into generative media, leveraging the same engineering discipline we apply in trading — versioned models, careful evaluation, and a strong focus on safety.

Lessons from Beats67 feed back into our client work on AI content pipelines, safety systems, and agentic media workflows.
