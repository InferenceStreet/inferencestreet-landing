Systematic Trading

Inference Street Alpha

An internally-operated, systematic trading program for US equities and equity index derivatives. Built in Python with robust execution, risk management, and continuous research.

Strategy in Plain Language

  • Diversified, rules-based signals: trend, mean reversion, volatility filters, and risk-parity style sizing.
  • Focus on liquid large and mid-cap US equities and major index products.
  • Strict risk limits per position, per day, and at the portfolio level.
  • Automatic drawdown controls and conservative leverage with modest gross exposure.
Performance context: In multi-year historical simulations, our flagship strategy shows low double-digit annualized returns, single-digit to low double-digit maximum drawdowns, and a Sharpe ratio comfortably above 1.0 depending on risk settings.
All performance figures are based on internal research and backtests. They are unaudited and for illustrative purposes only. Backtested results are not indicative of future performance. Live results will differ, sometimes materially. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

How We Build & Operate

  • Research stack: Python, modern data science libraries, and version-controlled experiments.
  • Data from reputable market providers; quality checks before research and live deployment.
  • Continuous integration for backtests and simulations to reduce drift and surprises.
  • Execution and risk monitoring with alerting, circuit breakers, and capital preservation first.

Prototype

Beats67 – AI Music Studio

Beats67 is our experimental AI music studio. It lets creators generate songs about their lives, interests, and stories — safely and playfully.

How it Works

  • Theme-based prompts (soccer, space, friendship, school, kindness, math) with guided creativity.
  • Content filters and safety controls.
  • Simple UI that favors playful exploration over complexity.
  • Focus on creativity and learning with songs about good habits and life principles.

Stage & Roadmap

Beats67 is currently in prototype. We are testing generation models, safety-forward UX flows, and controls with a small internal group.

Future directions include a mobile or web app, classroom-friendly modes, and personalized experiences like birthday songs or “life code” tracks.

Beats67 extends Inference Street’s capabilities beyond markets into generative media, leveraging the same engineering discipline we apply in trading — versioned models, careful evaluation, and a strong focus on safety.
